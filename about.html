About Us

Bridging the gap between good intentions and sustainable action.

The Motivation

We created this initiative because it was too easy to throw food away due to lack of organization. We want to make sustainability effortless. By using technology to track what we buy and suggesting recipes, we believe we can turn every household into a zero food waste champion.

Championing SDG 12

Aligned with UN Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production. Doing more with less.

Target 12.3

Halve per capita global food waste.

Target 12.5

Reduce waste generation substantially.

Target 12.8

Ensure sustainable development info.

Why We Promote It

Promoting SDG 12 is about survival. We aim to shift from "buy more" to "use better." Every time you cook a leftover meal or donate surplus food, you are actively contributing to this global goal.
